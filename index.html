Burnout Self-Test

Burnout is not a single switch that flips. It builds quietly, across three things: how drained you feel, how distant or cynical you have become, and how effective you still feel at what you do.

This is a short, honest self-check on where you are on that spectrum right now. About 4 minutes. You will get a personalised read at the end.
